Used SQL (Postgres) to explore the global covid data up until April 2021. The goal was to gain insights into how different 
countries suffered from the disease and how they are performing with vaccinations. The query results were then exported to Tableau Desktop for visualizations. 
This can be viewed at https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/devon.bennett/viz/CovidDashboard_16285327882330/Dashboard1
